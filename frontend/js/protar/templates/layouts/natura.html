Serra de Montejunto

SITECODE: PTCON0048

RELEASE_DA: 2013-02-06

MS: PT

SITETYPE: B

The “Serra de Montejunto” is a small limestone hill mostly of Jurassic origin; the dominant soils are derived of hard calcium carbonate rocks some with dolomitical or marl character. The calcareous soils are predominantly cambisoils and luvisoils.
