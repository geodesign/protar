Natura 2000

A paragraph about natura 2000. We propose the development of a web-based application where users can explore land cover change in protected areas across Europe. The application will quantify the current status and historical change in land cover within Europe's natural reserves. An intuitive interface with interactive graphs and maps will make this information understandable for everyone.
