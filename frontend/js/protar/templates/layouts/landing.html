Natura 2000

Over the last two decades Europe has grown one of the largest network of nature reserves in the world, protecting about 18% of land in EU countries. This network of protected areas is called Natura 2000. With Protar, we invite you to explore the current status and historical change in land cover in these areas.

Corine Land Cover

The Corine Land Cover Dataset is a map that describes the land cover for all of Europe in an unified framework. It describes what currently is located in any given area for most of Europe for four points in time: 1990, 2000, 2006, and 2012. We used this comprehensive dataset to calculate current and historic conditions of the Natura 2000 protected areas.
