Proposal

We propose the development of a web-based application where users can explore land cover change in protected areas across Europe. The application will quantify the current status and historical change in land cover within Europe's natural reserves. An intuitive interface with interactive graphs and maps will make this information understandable for everyone.

The goal is to provide information about what changes occurred within Europe's preserved areas and what their current state is. Over the last two decades Europe has grown one of the largest network of nature reserves in the world, protecting about 18% of land in EU countries. However, not all areas have management plans and there might be differences in enforcement of protection. The proposed application will provide important information to understand the dynamics within the protected areas and their surroundings.
